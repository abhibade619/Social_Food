## 2. The Problem
*   **Fragmented Discovery:** Food lovers rely on disjointed tools‚ÄîGoogle Maps for location, Instagram for photos, and Notes apps for lists.
*   **Lack of Trust:** Generic reviews from strangers lack the personal trust of recommendations from friends.
*   **Social Disconnect:** Dining is inherently social, but existing food apps are solitary experiences.

## 3. The Solution
**FoodSocial** is the first social network dedicated to your culinary life. It bridges the gap between discovery and social connection.
*   **See** where your friends are eating in real-time.
*   **Track** your own journey with a digital food diary.
*   **Discover** new spots based on trusted social signals, not just algorithms.

## 5. Technical Architecture (Tech Stack)

### üé® Frontend (The Experience)
*   **Framework:** **React.js** (via Vite) for a fast, responsive, and component-based UI.
*   **Styling:** **Vanilla CSS3** with a custom "Glassmorphism" design system.
    *   Features premium gradients, blur effects, and smooth animations.
    *   Fully responsive layout for desktop and mobile.
*   **Maps & Data:** **Google Maps JavaScript API** (Places Library, Geocoding) for rich location data and autocomplete.

### ‚öôÔ∏è Backend (The Engine)
*   **Platform:** **Supabase** (Open Source Firebase alternative).
*   **Database:** **PostgreSQL** for robust, relational data management (Users, Logs, Restaurants, Follows).
*   **Authentication:** **Supabase Auth** for secure Email/Password login and session management.
*   **Storage:** **Supabase Storage** for hosting user avatars and food photos.
